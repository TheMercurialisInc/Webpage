MINC Through The Years

2002

Mercurialis Inc. is founded on October 28th during EVE Online's beta phase, making us one of the oldest corporations in New Eden.

2005

MINC joins its first alliance, Interstellar Alcohol Conglomerate.

2008

MINC builds its first station, MINC Delirium, in RNF-YH (Catch). Later that year Against ALL Authorities conquers it. MINC leaves Interstellar Alcohol Conglomerate and joins Wildly Inappropriate.

2009

MINC builds a second station, MINC Aberration, in C-4ZOS (Branch).

2010

Looney Toons conquers MINC Aberration. MINC leaves Wildly Inappropriate and joins RAZOR Alliance. Isayo Arkindra steps down as CEO, appointing xMillz.

2012

xMillz steps down as CEO, appointing Nairb Ecrep.

2013

Battle of Asakai — one of EVE's most famous engagements, kicked off by a fleet commander who accidentally jumped his titan. MINC was on the field.

2014

Bloodbath of B-R5RB — the most famous battle in EVE history, triggered by an unpaid sovereignty bill. MINC answered the call.

2015

Nairb Ecrep steps down as CEO, appointing marly cortez.

2016

MINC leaves RAZOR Alliance and joins The Bastion, a member alliance of the Imperium. Casino War erupts — the war that toppled the Imperium at its peak. MINC fought through the entire campaign.

2018

marly cortez steps down as CEO, appointing primus buck. MINC is present for the Siege of 9-4RP2 and the Battle of X47L-Q, where 58 titans are destroyed in a single engagement.

2020

World War Bee 2 erupts across New Eden as the Imperium faces PAPI, a massive coalition assembled with one goal: destroy Goonswarm Federation. MINC served as a significant strategic asset in the war, coordinating a sustained campaign against TEST Alliance's backfields that resulted in the destruction of the most expensive Sotiyo ever killed at the time and the theft of approximately 400 billion ISK worth of structures. MINC was also present for the Fury at FWST-8, the current Guinness World Record holder for the largest PvP battle in gaming history, and the Massacre at M2-XFE, the most destructive battle in EVE history with 257 titans destroyed.

2021

primus buck steps down as CEO, appointing Khronos Thellere.

2022

MINC leaves The Bastion and joins Goonswarm Federation.

2024

Khronos Thellere steps down as CEO, appointing RVAMitchell.

2025

As a tribute to MINC's roots, the corporation anchors a faction fortizar in RNF-YH (Catch) — the same system where MINC Delirium once stood. The homecoming was short lived and MINC moved on again in 2026, but it was a fitting nod to where it all began.
