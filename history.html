MINC Through The Years

2002

Mercurialis Inc. is founded on October 28th during EVE Online's beta phase, making us one of the oldest corporations in New Eden.

2013

Battle of Asakai — one of EVE's most famous engagements, kicked off by a fleet commander who accidentally jumped his titan. MINC was on the field.

2014

Bloodbath of B-R5RB — the most famous battle in EVE history, triggered by an unpaid sovereignty bill. MINC answered the call.

2016

Casino War / World War Bee — the war that toppled the Imperium at its peak. MINC fought through the entire campaign.

2018

Siege of 9-4RP2 — a record breaking battle at the time. MINC was present for the fight.

2018

Battle of X47L-Q — 58 titans destroyed in a single engagement. MINC was on the field.

2020

Fury at FWST-8 — the current Guinness World Record holder for the largest PvP battle in gaming history. MINC was there.

2020

Massacre at M2-XFE — the most destructive battle in EVE history, 257 titans destroyed. MINC answered the call.
