MINC Through The Years

2002

Mercurialis Inc. is founded on October 28th during EVE Online's beta phase, making us one of the oldest corporations in New Eden.

2005

MINC joins its first alliance, Interstellar Alcohol Conglomerate (IAC), and establishes itself in Catch alongside their larger ally, Against All Authorities (AAA).

2008

MINC builds its first station, MINC Delirium, in RNF-YH (Catch). The relationship with AAA deteriorates as the larger alliance leverages its size to force IAC out of the region. During the conflict, two motherships (the precursor to today's supercarriers, exceedingly rare at the time) are lost, including reportedly the MINC Ragnarok. MINC leaves Interstellar Alcohol Conglomerate and joins Wildly Inappropriate.

2009

MINC builds a second station, MINC Aberration, in C-4ZOS (Branch).

2010

Looney Toons conquers MINC Aberration. MINC leaves Wildly Inappropriate and joins RAZOR Alliance. Isayo Arkindra steps down as CEO, appointing xMillz.

2011

MINC runs a Titans and Supercarriers class for EVE University, one of the game's premier teaching organizations. A recording of the class survives to this day.

2012

xMillz leaves the game and passes the CEO title to Nairb Ecrep. Critically, xMillz retains his corporation shares, a decision that will haunt the corp for years. During the RAZOR period, MINC conquers a station in P-E (Geminate), naming it De Ja Vu.

2013

The Battle of Asakai ignites on January 27th when a Goonswarm Titan pilot named Dabigredboat accidentally warps his ship directly into a Pandemic Legion fleet instead of bridging his own fleet to the field. What should have been a routine operation spirals into one of the largest supercapital engagements in EVE history as both the ClusterFuck Coalition and the HoneyBadger Coalition pour in reinforcements for hours.

The battle draws in 3,161 players across 270 alliances. When the smoke clears, the CFC has lost three Titans, five supercarriers, and 44 dreadnoughts. Total losses exceed 700 billion ISK, roughly $20,000 USD. MINC was on the field.

2014

The Bloodbath of B-R5RB erupts on January 27th, exactly one year after Asakai, when Pandemic Legion fails to pay a sovereignty bill for a staging system in Immensea. The CFC and Russian forces seize the opportunity and both sides commit their entire supercapital reserves in a battle lasting 21 hours. When it ends, 75 Titans lie destroyed alongside 13 supercarriers, 370 dreadnoughts, and 123 carriers. CCP later erects a permanent in-game monument in the system named "The Titanomachy." MINC answered the call.

2015

With Nairb Ecrep permanently AFK, the corp is in limbo and unable to move forward without reclaiming xMillz's shares. When contact is finally made, xMillz refuses to surrender them without compensation. In January 2015, MINC pays the price: approximately 100 billion ISK and all of the corp's super BPOs, assets locked away in an xMillz alt corporation. The settlement leaves MINC with almost nothing.

marly cortez takes over as CEO and immediately begins rebuilding. The timing proves fortunate as his son Rich holds a leadership position in RAZOR Alliance, giving MINC the footing it needs to recover.

2016

MINC leaves RAZOR Alliance and joins The Bastion, a member alliance of the Imperium. Shortly after, the Casino War erupts as a coalition of Imperium enemies, bankrolled by wealthy EVE gambling site operators, assembles the largest military mobilization the game has seen in years. The Moneybadgers Coalition drives the Imperium out of the north system by system. MINC fought through the entire campaign.

2018

marly cortez steps down as CEO, appointing primus buck. MINC is present for the Siege of 9-4RP2 and the Battle of X47L-Q, where 58 Titans are destroyed in a single engagement.

2020

World War Bee 2 erupts across New Eden as the Imperium faces PAPI, a massive coalition assembled with one goal: destroy Goonswarm Federation. MINC served as a significant strategic asset, coordinating a sustained campaign against TEST Alliance's backfields that resulted in the destruction of the most expensive Sotiyo ever killed at the time and the theft of approximately 400 billion ISK worth of structures.

MINC was also present for the Fury at FWST-8, the current Guinness World Record holder for the largest PvP battle in gaming history, and the Massacre at M2-XFE, the most destructive battle in EVE history with 257 Titans destroyed.

2021

primus buck steps down as CEO, appointing Khronos Thellere.

2022

MINC leaves The Bastion and joins Goonswarm Federation.

2024

Khronos Thellere steps down as CEO, appointing RVAMitchell. As operations expand, MINC anchors a Fortizar in ZTS named De Ja Vu, a quiet tribute to the station conquered in Geminate over a decade prior.

2025

As a tribute to MINC's roots, the corporation anchors a faction Fortizar in RNF-YH (Catch), the same system where MINC Delirium once stood, naming it MINC Delirium in honor of the corp's first home. The homecoming was short lived and MINC moved on again in 2026, but it was a fitting nod to where it all began.
