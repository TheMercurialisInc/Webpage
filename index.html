Welcome to Mercurialis Inc.

Established in 2002 during EVE Online's beta, MINC is the oldest active corporation in New Eden with over two decades of good times and good fights. We have been present for every major conflict the game has seen. From the chaos of Asakai, to the Bloodbath of B-R5RB, through the Casino War, the Siege of 9-4RP2, the titan graveyard at X47L-Q, and into the record-breaking Fury at FWST-8 and the Massacre at M2-XFE .

Our playstyles range from wormhole exploration to capital production, small BLOPs gangs to large scale fleet fights.

MINC is home to players from all over the world, across time zones and backgrounds. Our community is multicultural and inclusive, and that diversity is a genuine strength and makes for great conversation and environment where every member's unique perspective and contribution is valued.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or new to the game, MINC offers something most corporations can't: a genuine community with real history behind it, and plenty of content ahead of it.
