Welcome to Mercurialis Inc.

Established in 2002 during EVE Online's beta phase, MINC has become the epitome of longevity and success in nullsec PvP. With a remarkable 90% kill efficiency rate spanning over 21 years, MINC has solidified its position as the longest-standing corporation in this arena.

Comprising approximately 150 pilots spread across various timezones, MINC maintains a robust presence in EVE's nullsec landscape. From small-scale skirmishes to large capital engagements, its members exhibit a diverse range of skills and expertise, ensuring formidable performance on the battlefield.

What sets MINC apart is its vibrant and inclusive community, characterized by a mature and drama-free atmosphere. Whether engaging in PvP, mining, or ratting, members benefit from experienced leadership, extensive logistical support, and a generous Ship Replacement Program (SRP).

Joining MINC means embracing a legacy of excellence and camaraderie. With a commitment to providing both seasoned veterans and newcomers with ample opportunities for combat and fun, MINC remains a beacon of resilience and determination in the ever-evolving universe of EVE Online.
