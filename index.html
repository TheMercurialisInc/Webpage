About MINC

Founded on October 28, 2002 during the beta of EVE Online, Mercurialis Inc. (MINC) is the oldest active corporation in New Eden. For more than two decades, MINC has adapted through every era of EVE, from the early days of nullsec warfare, through the age of supercapital battles, and into the modern era of alliance warfare.

Our pilots have fought in some of the game's defining conflicts, including Asakai, B-R5RB, the Casino War, X47L-Q, FWST-8, and M2-XFE. From massive coalition battles to small-gang fights, MINC has always found a place on the battlefield.

Beyond combat, MINC has built a lasting community around exploration, industry, capital production, and the friendships formed along the way. Our members come from around the world, bringing together veterans with decades of experience and new pilots writing their first chapters.

More than twenty years later, MINC remains active because of the people who continue to shape its story.And there is still history to be written.
