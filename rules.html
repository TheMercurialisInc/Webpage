± Join Mercurialis Inc.

Founded in 2002 during the beta of EVE Online, Mercurialis Inc. is one of New Eden's oldest corporations and has been part of the game's history from the very beginning.

For more than two decades, MINC has adapted through the constant evolution of EVE, from the earliest days of Eve Online, through the rise and fall of alliances, and into some of the largest conflicts ever fought in New Eden.

Across generations of pilots, Mercurialis Inc. has built, fought, explored, produced, and adapted. We have stood on the front lines of wars, supported the industrial efforts that kept alliances moving, and experienced nearly every side of life in New Eden.

Today, we are looking for new pilots to help write the next chapter.

Who We Are

Mercurialis Inc. is a nullsec corporation built around experienced veterans, new pilots, and the community that connects them.

Our members have fought major wars, built industrial operations, produced ships for major conflicts, explored unknown space, and taken part in the countless moments that make EVE Online unlike any other universe.

But beyond killboards, campaigns, and battle reports, MINC has always been about the people behind the characters.

We believe the strongest corporations are built by pilots who have a reason to log in, people to fly with, and a place where they can continue learning, contributing, and growing.

± Life in Mercurialis Inc.

EVE is not just about ships and systems. It is about the people you fly with, the decisions you make, and the stories created along the way.

MINC members participate across many areas of New Eden:

  • Alliance warfare and fleet operations
  • Small gang PvP and roaming
  • Industry and production
  • Exploration and PvE
  • Supporting the infrastructure that keeps our operations moving

Whether you are learning nullsec for the first time, returning after years away, or looking for a corporation with history and stability, MINC provides a place to get involved.

What You Can Expect
  • A corporation with more than two decades of history and a future still being written.
  • Active pilots across different time zones with opportunities to fly together.
  • Experienced members willing to help new and returning pilots understand modern EVE.
  • The infrastructure needed to operate in nullsec, including logistics, industry support, and corporation programs.
  • A community that values participation over simply filling a roster.
Who We Are Looking For

MINC is looking for pilots who want more than a corporation ticker beside their name.

We are looking for people who want to log in, fly with others, contribute, and become part of the community.

You do not need to be a veteran. You do not need to know every mechanic in EVE.

You do need a good attitude, respect for others, and a willingness to participate.

± Why MINC?

After more than twenty years, Mercurialis Inc. is still here because generations of pilots chose to build something together.

The universe has changed. Alliances have risen and fallen. Wars have reshaped New Eden.

Through it all, MINC has remained what it has always been: a group of pilots flying together and creating stories that only EVE can produce.

A Few Things We Provide

New and returning pilots are welcome. Experienced members can help with:

  • Ship fitting
  • Skill planning
  • ISK generation
  • Nullsec survival
  • Understanding modern EVE mechanics

EVE has changed a lot over the years. We help pilots catch up and get involved.

± How To Join

The process is simple:

  1. Join our in-game channel: MINC BAR
  2. Introduce yourself to a recruiter or director.
  3. Have a conversation with us and learn more about the corporation.
  4. Authenticate through SeAT and complete onboarding.

Recruitment fees remain at 0 ISK. Bribes are still accepted and appreciated.

± Requirements
Membership

MINC has survived for more than two decades because we take trust, respect, and community seriously. The CEO and directorate reserve the right to remove members if trust is violated or conduct damages the corporation.

Attitude

MINC is a multicultural and inclusive corporation. Bigotry, harassment, and deliberate toxicity are not accepted.

Activity

We do not require 24/7 activity. Real life comes first, but members should remain reasonably active and communicate if they will be away for extended periods.

Fleet Participation

Mandatory participation applies only to strategic alliance operations. Regular fleets are voluntary — but the more you participate, the more opportunities you will find.

Security Requirements

Members are required to:

  • Use SeAT authentication
  • Provide required ESI access
  • Join corporation Discord
