About MINC

Established in October 2002 during EVE Online's closed beta, Mercurialis Inc. holds the distinction of being the oldest continuously active corporation in New Eden. Over two decades of history, hard fights, and good times bind this community together.

Our playstyles range from wormhole exploration to capital production, small BLOPs gangs to large-scale titan brawls. MINC is home to players from every time zone and background.
