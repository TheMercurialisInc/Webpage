Our History: A Legacy of Perseverance

Founded during EVE Online’s beta phase, Mercurialis Inc. has been a part of New Eden from the very beginning. Our history is one of steady adaptation and perseverance, shaped by the evolving challenges and opportunities of the universe. Featured in the Prima Official Strategy Guide, our roots run deep, and our journey has taken us through some of the most defining moments in the game’s history.

Over the years, we have been proud members of alliances such as the Interstellar Alcohol Conglomerate (IAC), Razor Alliance, The Bastion, and now Goonswarm Federation. Each chapter has brought new experiences, challenges, and friendships, helping us grow and refine our approach to life in New Eden. Whether participating in large-scale conflicts or focusing on industry and exploration, our focus has always been on adapting and working together.

Today, Mercurialis Inc. continues as a diverse and close-knit community, shaped by our long-standing commitment to teamwork and evolution. Our legacy is not defined by boasting of accomplishments, but by the relationships we've built, the lessons we've learned, and our dedication to remaining an integral part of EVE's ever-changing landscape.
