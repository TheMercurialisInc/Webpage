Recruitment: Join the Ranks of Mercurialis Inc.

Becoming a part of Mercurialis Inc. means stepping into a legacy of excellence, camaraderie, and adventure that spans over two decades. Whether you're a battle-hardened veteran or a curious newcomer, there's a place for you within our ranks. MINC is more than a corporation; it's a family of like-minded pilots who share a passion for exploring, conquering, and thriving in the vast universe of EVE Online.

If you’re ready to forge your legacy among the stars, you’ve come to the right place. This page outlines everything you need to know about joining MINC, what we expect from you, and what you can expect from us.

Why Join Mercurialis Inc.?

Mercurialis Inc. is a corporation built on the principles of unity, diversity, and resilience. We offer an unparalleled experience for pilots of all skill levels, with a focus on community, growth, and achievement. Here’s what sets us apart:

  • Legacy: Established in 2002, MINC is one of the longest-running corporations in EVE Online, with a history of success and adaptation.
  • Diverse Playstyles: From PvP battles and wormhole exploration to industry and capital production, we embrace a wide range of activities.
  • Supportive Community: Our members are our strength. We provide guidance, training, and opportunities for pilots of all experience levels.
  • Cutting-Edge Tools: Gain access to powerful tools like the Eve Rift Intel Fusion Tool (RIFT) to enhance your gameplay.
  • Opportunities for Growth: Whether you’re looking to lead fleets, master industry, or simply have fun, MINC provides the platform for success.

Recruitment Process

Joining Mercurialis Inc. is a straightforward process designed to ensure you’re ready to thrive in our community. Here’s how it works:

  1. Step 1: Express Interest – Join our in-game channel “MINC BAR” or connect with us on Discord. Introduce yourself and let us know why you’re interested in joining.
  2. Step 2: Application – Complete our recruitment application via SeAT. This process includes ESI verification, which ensures transparency and security.
  3. Step 3: Interview – Our recruitment team will conduct a brief interview to understand your goals, playstyle, and how you’d like to contribute to MINC.
  4. Step 4: Relocation – Once accepted, you’ll receive instructions on moving to our staging system and integrating into the corporation.
  5. Step 5: Integration – Dive into fleet operations, join the conversation in Discord, and start making your mark in New Eden!

*Bribes to expedite your application are optional but encouraged. (Capsuleers who show initiative are always noticed!)

What We Expect from Recruits

Joining MINC is not just about what you can gain—it’s about how you contribute to the greater good of the corporation. Here’s what we expect from every member:

  • Teamwork: Be an active participant in fleets, discussions, and operations. Together, we’re stronger.
  • Respect: Treat your fellow members, allies, and even enemies with respect. Toxicity has no place in MINC.
  • Preparedness: Familiarize yourself with our doctrines and ensure you’re always ready for fleet deployment.
  • Communication: Stay active in our Discord server and in-game channels. Communication is the key to success.
  • Initiative: Whether it’s proposing new ideas or taking the lead in operations, we value members who aren’t afraid to step up.

What You Can Expect from Us

Mercurialis Inc. is committed to providing a supportive and rewarding environment for all its members. Here’s what you can expect when you join:

  • A Thriving Community: Join a group of dedicated and experienced pilots who are always ready to help and share knowledge.
  • Regular Operations: Participate in scheduled PvP roams, mining fleets, training sessions, and more.
  • Access to Resources: Take advantage of corporate resources, including fleet doctrines, training programs, and intel tools.
  • Opportunities for Advancement: Rise through the ranks, take on leadership roles, and leave your mark on New Eden.

Policy on Espionage

At Mercurialis Inc., we maintain an unwavering stance against espionage. Spies are strictly prohibited from joining our ranks under any circumstances. We value integrity, transparency, and the security of our operations above all else. Joining Mercurialis Inc. under false pretenses undermines these principles and will not be tolerated.

That being said, we recognize the diverse needs of the EVE Online community. If your path is one of covert operations and intelligence gathering, we strongly encourage you to consider our esteemed friends at -GEN-, UVIM, CRVOS (for our Spanish-speaking allies), OMZI (for our Slavic brethren), or the respected organization ADFU. These corporations are known for their accommodating recruitment processes and may align better with your specialized skillset.

For those spies who feel compelled to join Mercurialis Inc. despite our explicit policy, it is imperative that you adhere to the following protocol:

  • Declare your intentions: Notify your recruiter immediately of your status as a spy and provide the name and contact details of your handler.
  • Follow standard procedures: Complete the recruitment application via SeAT with full ESI verification to ensure transparency.
  • Operate with integrity: Maintain open communication regarding your activities to foster a respectful relationship during your time with us.

Failure to disclose espionage activities will result in immediate action, including but not limited to expulsion, blacklisting, and the strategic sharing of any relevant intel with interested parties. Mercurialis Inc. does not tolerate deception within its ranks unless explicitly declared and authorized.

We appreciate your cooperation in this matter and look forward to working with you in the spirit of mutual understanding and respect, should your circumstances meet the above criteria.
