Resources: Your Toolkit for New Eden

The universe of EVE Online is vast and complex, but you don’t have to navigate it alone. Mercurialis Inc. has compiled a list of essential resources to help you excel in every aspect of the game, from PvP to industry, from intelligence gathering to community events. These tools and services have been vetted by our pilots and are considered invaluable for success in New Eden.

1. Pyfa: Ship Fitting Tool

Pyfa (Python Fitting Assistant) is the go-to tool for planning and optimizing your ship fits. It allows you to experiment with different modules, rigs, and skills to see how they affect your ship's performance without committing in-game resources.

  • Key Features: Detailed ship stats, integration with character skills, and module comparisons.
  • Download: GitHub Repository

2. Dotlan

Dotlan is an indispensable map tool for navigating New Eden. It provides detailed maps of all regions, jump bridges, sovereignty, and system statistics.

  • Use Case: Planning fleet routes, finding trade hubs, and tracking sovereignty changes.
  • Website: evemaps.dotlan.net

3. ZKillboard

ZKillboard is the definitive tool for tracking kills and losses in EVE Online. Use it to analyze enemy fleet compositions, track your corporation's performance, or scout for potential targets.

  • Use Case: Intel gathering, PvP analysis, and bragging rights.
  • Website: zkillboard.com

4. Eve Rift Intel Fusion Tool (RIFT)

Designed by Mercurialis Inc.’s own Nohus, RIFT is our official intel tool for staying ahead in New Eden. RIFT allows pilots to manage and analyze intel efficiently, giving us a strategic edge in every operation.

  • Download: RIFT Website
  • Source Code: GitLab Repository

5. aDashboard

aDashboard is an invaluable web-based tool for managing your in-game assets, finances, and production lines. It's particularly useful for industrialists and trade-focused pilots.

  • Use Case: Asset tracking, market analysis, and production planning.
  • Website: adashboard.info

6. Industry Tools

Managing industry in EVE requires precise planning and the right tools. Here are some of the best tools for industrialists:

  • EVE ISK Per Hour (IPH): A comprehensive tool for calculating profitability and managing industry projects. Learn More
  • Fuzzwork Industry Tools: Covers everything from blueprint calculators to market hubs. Learn More

7. ITL

ITL is the gold standard for hauling services in New Eden. Whether you're moving assets across highsec or lowsec, their trusted service ensures your goods arrive safely.

  • Website: NEED TO FIND ITL URL

8. Best of US (Military Veterans Group)

Best of US is a community group specifically for military veterans who play EVE Online. They provide camaraderie, support, and a shared space for those who’ve served.

  • Website:

9. EVE Online Devblogs

Stay up to date with official news, patch notes, and announcements directly from CCP through their developer blogs.

  • Website: EVE Devblogs
